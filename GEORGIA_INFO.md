# Georgia-Specific Company Formation Details

> US residence state: Georgia. Supplements COMPANY_STEPS.md.

---

## Georgia + Delaware LLC (if forming in Delaware)

- Georgia requires **foreign LLC registration** to operate from the state
- Filing with Georgia Secretary of State: **$225** (Annual Registration) + **$50** foreign LLC filing fee
- Georgia has **state income tax** (5.39% flat rate) — LLC pass-through income is taxable in Georgia regardless of where the LLC is formed
- Annual costs: Delaware franchise tax ($300/yr) + Delaware registered agent ($50-150/yr) + Georgia foreign qualification ($225 initial + $50/yr annual registration)

## Georgia LLC (alternative — recommended for pre-revenue)

- Filing fee: **$100** with Georgia Secretary of State
- No Delaware franchise tax, no registered agent fee, no foreign qualification needed
- Can still convert to a Delaware C-Corp later if raising VC
- For a solo founder pre-revenue, this saves ~$400-500/yr in overhead

## Recommended Path

At pre-revenue solo founder stage: **Georgia LLC ($100) + Mercury bank account (free) + Stripe (free to set up)** is the pragmatic move. Save the Delaware formality for when raising or when enterprise customers care.
