## Purpose

This structure represents the **minimum functional coverage** required
to operate a semi‑autonomous software or AI‑driven organization.

Together these roles cover the four operational pillars of a modern
company:

-   Strategy and Decision Making
-   Engineering and Delivery
-   Intelligence and Knowledge
-   Business and Operations

This blueprint can be used to assign AI agents, human roles, or hybrid
responsibilities within an AI‑augmented organization.
